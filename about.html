About Us

Casbah Restaurant is a Mediterranean/Middle Eastern restaurant. We are locally owned and managed by Isam Showli and Omar Moon.
Omar is a native of Agadir, Morocco and is also our head chef. Isam has more than 20 years of experience in the restaurant business.
He also owned and managed several local establishments. We offer traditional favorites such as Kabobs, Moussaka, Spanakopita, Baba Ganoush, Hummus, Falafel, Shawerma and Gyro.

Whether it's a dinner for two or for a special occassion we can provide seating. We look forward to having you as our guest.
We are located at 1890 N. Germantown Parkway, on the east side of the street between Dexter and Woodchase.
